### Step 1: Create a Facebook Developer Account

To access the WhatsApp Business API, you'll first need a Facebook Developer account.

1. Go to the [Facebook Developer Portal](https://developers.facebook.com/).
2. Click **Get Started** or **My Apps**.
3. If you don’t have an account, click **Sign Up** and follow the instructions.
4. Go to **My Apps** > **Create App**.

## Step 2: Create a New App for WhatsApp

1. Choose **Business** as the app type.
2. Enter an app name (e.g., `My WhatsApp Integration`).
3. Select or create a **Business Account**.
4. Click **Create App**.
![Phone Verification Screenshot](/images/create_app.png)

## Step 3: Add the WhatsApp Business API Product

1. In your app dashboard, click **Add Product**.
2. Find **WhatsApp** and click **Set Up**.

## Step 4: Link Your WhatsApp Business Account

1. On the WhatsApp setup page, click **Link WhatsApp Account**.
2. Log in if prompted.
3. Choose your WhatsApp Business Account or create a new one.

## Step 5: Verify Your Phone Number

1. Go to the **Phone Numbers** section.
2. Click **Add Phone Number**.
3. Enter and verify your number via SMS or voice call.

## Step 6: Set Up Webhooks

1. Go to **Configuration** under the **WhatsApp** product.
2. Click **Setup Webhooks**.
3. Enter your webhook URL.  
   Example: `https://yourdomain.com/webhook`
4. Generate and save a **Verify Token**.
5. Click **Verify and Save**.

## Step 7: Go Live

1. Test message sending and receiving.
2. Monitor your webhook logs.

✅ You’re all set! Your WhatsApp number is now integrated and ready to use.
